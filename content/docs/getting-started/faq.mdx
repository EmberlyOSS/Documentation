## Account & Sign Up

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit [embrly.ca](https://embrly.ca) and click **"Sign Up"**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email (click the link we send you)

You start on the **Spark plan** (10 GB free) with no credit card required.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Go to **Settings → Billing**:

- **Upgrade** — instant, pro-rated for the remainder of your billing cycle
- **Downgrade** — takes effect at the next renewal date
- **Cancel** — anytime, no lock-in

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page and click **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click the link in the email we send
4. Set a new password

### How do I delete my account?

**Settings → Account → Delete Account**, then enter your password to confirm.

<Callout type="warn">
  Account deletion is permanent. All files and data are deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
</Callout>

## Files & Uploads

### What file types are supported?

Most file types are accepted. The following are blocked for security reasons:

- Executable files (`.exe`, `.bat`, `.sh`, `.ps1`, etc. when uploaded directly)
- Files that trigger malware detection by VirusTotal

### What are the upload size limits?

| Plan | Max single upload |
|------|-------------------|
| Spark | 500 MB |
| Glow | 1 GB |
| Flare | 2 GB |
| Blaze | 5 GB |
| Inferno | 10 GB |
| Ember | Unlimited |

Files larger than your plan limit must be split or upgraded.

### Can I password-protect a file?

Yes. When uploading or by editing an existing file, set a password. Anyone accessing the file will need to enter it. Passwords are hashed server-side — we never see them in plain text.

### Can I set a file to auto-delete?

Yes. Set an **expiration date** on upload or afterward via the edit dialog. The file is automatically deleted at the set time. You'll receive an email reminder 2 days before expiration.

### How do I share a private file with someone?

Add them as a **collaborator**:

1. Open the file details
2. Click **"Add Collaborator"**
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose **View** or **Edit** permission

They'll receive an email invite. You can revoke access at any time.

## Security & Privacy

### Do you scan uploaded files?

Yes. Every upload is scanned using a **VirusTotal hash lookup** against 71+ antivirus engines. The file content itself is never sent to VirusTotal — only its SHA-256 hash. See [VirusTotal Integration](/docs/security/virustotal) for details.

### What is the HIBP check at login?

When you sign in, Emberly checks whether your password appears in known data breach databases via [HaveIBeenPwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com/). Your password is never sent — only the first 5 characters of its SHA-1 hash. If found, you're warned and given the option to change your password. See [HIBP Integration](/docs/security/hibp) for details.

### Is my data encrypted?

Yes:

- Files are encrypted at rest in storage
- All connections use HTTPS (TLS)
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt
- File passwords use server-side hashing

## Custom Domains

### How many custom domains can I have?

Your limit is based on your plan, plus any bonuses from perks:

| Plan | Base Domains |
|------|-------------|
| Spark | 3 |
| Glow | 6 |
| Flare | 12 |
| Blaze | 25 |

Contributors and Discord boosters can earn additional slots. See [Domain Perks](/docs/user-guide/contributor-perks).

### How do I verify a custom domain?

Add a CNAME record pointing your domain to `cname.embrly.ca`, then click **"Verify"** in your dashboard. TLS is provisioned automatically via Cloudflare. See the [Custom Domains guide](/docs/user-guide/custom-domains) for full instructions.

## Billing

### What payment methods are accepted?

Credit and debit cards via Stripe. More methods may be added in future.

### Do you offer refunds?

Contact [support@embrly.ca](mailto:support@embrly.ca) within 7 days of a charge for billing issues.

### Do you offer student or non-profit discounts?

Not currently. Check the [pricing page](https://embrly.ca/pricing) for any active promotions.
