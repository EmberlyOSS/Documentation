## Individual Plans

### Spark (Free)

Perfect for getting started with file sharing.

**Features:**
- 10 GB storage
- Upload files up to 500 MB
- 3 custom domains
- Password protection
- Expiration dates
- Basic analytics
- OCR on all uploads
- Public and private sharing
- Download tracking

**Best for:** Individuals, students, casual users

---

### Glow ($4.99/month)

First paid tier with more storage and uploads.

**Features:**
- 25 GB storage
- Upload files up to 1 GB
- 5 custom domains
- Everything in Spark, plus:
- Standard analytics (per-file view counts)
- File revision history

**Best for:** Content creators, small teams sharing occasionally

---

### Flare ($8.99/month)

Advanced storage for serious sharing.

**Features:**
- 50 GB storage
- Upload files up to 2 GB
- 10 custom domains
- Everything in Glow, plus:
- Advanced analytics
- Batch file operations
- Enhanced API rate limits

**Best for:** Professionals, designers, media creators

---

### Blaze ($14.99/month)

Team collaboration with unlimited domains.

**Features:**
- 100 GB storage
- Upload files up to 5 GB
- Unlimited custom domains
- Everything in Flare, plus:
- Team features (up to 5 team members)
- Shared workspaces
- Team analytics
- Role-based access control

**Best for:** Small teams, offices, collaborative projects

---

## Premium Individual Plans

### Inferno ($24.99/month)

Enterprise-grade storage for power users.

**Features:**
- 250 GB storage
- Upload files up to 10 GB
- Unlimited custom domains
- Everything in Blaze, plus:
- Extended team support (up to 10 members)
- Workspace templates
- Advanced team settings
- Priority support

**Best for:** Large teams, agencies, enterprises

---

### Ember ($49.99/month)

Unlimited everything for serious operations.

**Features:**
- Unlimited storage
- Unlimited upload size
- Unlimited custom domains
- Unlimited team members
- Everything in Inferno, plus:
- SSO/SAML support
- Advanced security options
- Webhook integrations
- Custom branding options (enterprise)

**Best for:** Large organizations, agencies, professional services

---

### Enterprise (Custom Pricing)

Fully customized solution with dedicated support.

**Features:**
- Unlimited storage
- Unlimited upload size
- Unlimited everything
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority support
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantees
- On-premise/hybrid deployment options

**Best for:** Fortune 500 companies, government, highly regulated industries

## Security & Compliance

All plans include:
- VirusTotal scanning (71+ antivirus engines)
- Password protection
- File expiration dates
- HTTPS/TLS encryption
- HIBP password breach checking
- Two-factor authentication (TOTP)
- Bcrypt password hashing
- File versioning

**Inferno+ adds:**
- Advanced audit logs
- IP whitelisting
- Custom retention policies
- SOC 2 compliance support

**Enterprise includes:**
- Full HIPAA support
- FedRAMP certification
- Custom data residency
- Audit trails with retention
- Compliance reporting

## Billing Details

### Monthly Billing
All plans renew on the same date each month. Cancel anytime with no penalty.

### Annual Billing
Available on all paid plans with 20% discount. Renews automatically after 12 months.

### Upgrade/Downgrade
- **Upgrades** are instant and pro-rated to your billing cycle
- **Downgrades** take effect at your next renewal date

### Free Trial
Spark plan requires no credit card. Paid plans offer a 7-day trial.

## Adding Extra Resources

### Additional Storage
Purchase extra storage in 100 GB increments for any plan.

### Additional Domains
Add custom domain slots beyond your plan limit.

### Additional Team Members
Increase your team capacity with single-seat licenses ($2.99/month per seat).

## Choosing the Right Plan

**Just getting started?** Start with Spark (free) — no credit card needed.

**Sharing files regularly?** Glow gives you 25 GB and domain customization.

**Need to share large files or work in teams?** Jump to Flare or Blaze.

**Running a business or agency?** Inferno or Ember offer team features and unlimited capacity.

**Need enterprise features?** Contact us about custom Enterprise plans.

---

## Upgrading to a Paid Plan

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing → Plans**
2. Click **Upgrade** on the plan you want
3. Enter payment details
4. Start using your new plan immediately

Your new limits take effect instantly.
