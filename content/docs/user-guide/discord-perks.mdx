## How to Unlock

**1. Join the Discord server**

Join at [discord.gg/k2QAfkwDwK](https://discord.gg/k2QAfkwDwK).

**2. Boost the server**

In Discord, right-click the Emberly server icon → **"Server Boost"** → apply your boost.

**3. Link your Discord account**

1. Go to **Dashboard → Profile → Linked Accounts**
2. Click **"Link Discord Account"**
3. Authorize the OAuth connection

**4. Perks are applied automatically**

Benefits are applied within 24 hours of linking. Check your tier in **Dashboard → Profile → Perks**.
