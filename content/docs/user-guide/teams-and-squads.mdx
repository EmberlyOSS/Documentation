## What is Nexium?

Nexium is Emberly's team collaboration layer that lets you:

- Create named teams (called "squads")
- Invite team members with specific roles
- Organize files in shared workspaces
- Manage permissions at the team level
- Track team activity and usage
- Scale from small teams to large organizations

## Discovery Plans (Team Subscriptions)

For teams without individual Blaze+ subscriptions, Emberly offers **Discovery plans** — shared team subscriptions:

| Plan | Max Members | Storage | Price |
|------|-------------|---------|-------|
| Discovery Free | 3 | 5 GB | Free |
| Discovery Pro | 10 | 20 GB | $8/mo |
| Discovery Team | 25 | 50 GB | $20/mo |
| Discovery Studio | Unlimited | Unlimited | $50/mo |

Discovery plans are shared team subscriptions. All team members share the same storage pool.

## Key Concepts

### Squad
A named team in Nexium. Examples: "Design Team", "Product", "Finance", etc.

### Members
People invited to your squad with role-based permissions.

### Workspace
Shared folder where squad members organize and collaborate on files.

### Roles
- **Owner** — Full control, manage members, delete squad
- **Admin** — Manage members, workspaces, files
- **Editor** — Create and edit files, invite members
- **Viewer** — Download and view only

## Quick Start

### Create a Squad

1. Go to **Dashboard → Teams → Create Squad**
2. Enter a name and description
3. Choose your team size
4. Click **Create**

### Invite Members

1. Go to **Squad Settings → Members**
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter their email and choose a role
4. They'll receive an email invite

### Create a Workspace

1. Go to your squad dashboard
2. Click **"New Workspace"**
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Q2 Campaign Assets")
4. Set member access level
5. Click **Create**

## Common Workflows

### Sales Team File Sharing

Use a squad to share sales collateral (presentations, case studies, pricing sheets) with everyone on the sales team.

1. Create a squad called "Sales"
2. Add all sales reps
3. Create workspaces for "Pitch Decks", "Case Studies", "Product Sheets"
4. Upload assets to respective workspaces
5. Everyone stays in sync with latest versions

### Design Agency Project Delivery

Structure projects per-client with dedicated workspaces.

1. Create a squad per client (or one squad with multiple workspaces per client)
2. Assign designers and PMs to the squad
3. Create workspaces for each project phase or deliverable
4. Clients can join as "Viewers" for review access

### Open Source Project Coordination

Organize community contributions and releases.

1. Create a squad for your project
2. Add core contributors
3. Create workspaces for "Releases", "Assets", "Documentation"
4. Grant community viewer access to approved materials

## Advanced Features

### Nested Workspaces
Create sub-workspaces to organize complex projects:
```
Project Alpha
├── Design
│   ├── Wireframes
│   ├── Mockups
│   └── Final Assets
├── Development
│   └── Source Code
└── Marketing
    ├── Copy
    └── Promotional Assets
```

### Role-Based Access
Set different permissions per workspace:
- Designers get edit access to Design workspace
- Developers get edit access to Development workspace
- Everyone gets viewer access to Marketing workspace

### Team Activity Log
View all squad activity:
- Who uploaded what
- When files were accessed
- Member additions/removals
- Permission changes

### Team Analytics
Monitor your squad's usage:
- Storage by workspace
- Upload frequency
- Team member activity
- Most active workspaces

## Billing

### Individual Team Plans (Blaze+)

If you have a Blaze+ plan, team features are already included. Add more team members anytime (if within your plan limit).

### Discovery Shared Team Plans

Discovery plans are separate subscriptions shared by the entire team:
- Any team member can manage the subscription
- Billing is per squad, not per person
- Upgrade or downgrade team-wide

### Calculating Costs

**Option 1: Individual Plans**
- 5 people on Blaze ($14.99/mo each) = $74.95/mo
- Total: $74.95/mo for 5 with 100 GB total storage

**Option 2: Discovery Team**
- 5-25 people on Discovery Team ($20/mo) = $20/mo
- Total: $20/mo for up to 25 with 50 GB shared storage

## Troubleshooting

### Member Not Seeing Files
- Check they were invited to the correct workspace
- Verify their role has access to that workspace
- Ask them to refresh their dashboard

### Can't Invite More Members
- Check your plan limit (Blaze: 5, Inferno: 10, Ember/Enterprise: unlimited)
- Consider upgrading or using a Discovery plan
- Contact support if limits seem incorrect

### Workspace Not Syncing
- Refresh your browser
- Check network connection
- Wait a few seconds — syncing may be in progress
- Contact support if issue persists
