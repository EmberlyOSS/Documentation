## What is Emberly?

Emberly is a lightning-fast file hosting and sharing platform that puts you in control. Upload files once, share anywhere, and track who accesses what.

### Key Highlights

- **Fast** — Sub-second uploads and downloads with global CDN
- **Secure** — Malware scanning with 71+ antivirus engines, password protection, and encryption
- **Private** — Your files belong to you — no ads, no tracking
- **Flexible** — Works with your tools via API, CLI, integrations, or web dashboard
- **Open Source** — Fully open source on [GitHub](https://github.com/EmberlyOSS/Website)
