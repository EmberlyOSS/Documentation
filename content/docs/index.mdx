## What is Emberly?

Emberly is a modern, secure file hosting and sharing platform that puts you in control. Upload files once, share anywhere, and track who accesses what.

**Available as:**
- **Cloud-hosted** — Use our managed service (7 plans from free to enterprise)
- **Self-hosted** — Deploy on your own infrastructure (fully open source)

### Key Highlights

- **Fast** — Sub-second uploads and downloads with global CDN
- **Secure** — Malware scanning with 71+ antivirus engines, password protection, and encryption
- **Private** — Your files belong to you — no ads, no tracking
- **Flexible** — Works with your tools via API, CLI, integrations, or web dashboard
- **Open Source** — Fully open source on [GitHub](https://github.com/EmberlyOSS/Emberly)
- **Team-Ready** — Built-in collaboration features for teams of any size
- **Scalable** — From individual creators to enterprise organizations
